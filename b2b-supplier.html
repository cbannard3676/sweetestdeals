Company Overview

Shishi Xinjia Electronics Co. Ltd.

Shishi Xinjia Electronics Co., Ltd. was established in July 1997, with a construction area of over 30,000 square meters of garden-style standard factory located in Baogai Technology Industrial Zone, Shishi City, Fujian Province, China. It is a private enterprise integrated development, manufacture and trade of cold light sports watch, talking watch, calculator watch, electronic watch and electronic clock, analog digital watches, quartz watches, etc.

Business Type: Manufacturer, Trading Company
Location: Fujian, China (Mainland)
Year Established: 2001
Total Revenue: US$10 Million - US$50 Million
Main Products: XINJIA brand LCD sport watch,LCD digital clock
Main Markets: Domestic Market, Central America, Western Europe, Mid East, South America, North America
